Doose's Market

About Doose's Market

Doose's is the grocery store in Stars Hollow, owned by Taylor Doose, the town Selectman. Taylor is protective of his business throughout the series complaining on several occasions when he believes it threatened. It is the only grocery store in Stars Hollow. The market has been a staple in Stars Hollow for many years, serving as the primary grocery store for residents. It's a family-owned business that has been passed down through generations of the Doose family.

TYPE: Grocery Shop
PROPRIETOR: Taylor Doose
MANAGER(S): Taylor Doose (owner)
STAFF: Kirk Gleason (assistant manager) & Dean Forester (former bag boy)
AMENITIES: Food & Drink
LOCATION: Stars Hollow
