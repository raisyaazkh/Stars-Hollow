Weston Bakery

About Weston Bakery

Weston's Bakery is a local bakery ran by the Weston Family of Stars Hollow. It is known for serving lots of cakes and having really good pie.

BUSINESS: Weston's Bakery
TYPE: Bakery
PROPRIETOR: Fran Weston
Est: 1889
STAFF: Fran Weston (owner) & Kirk Gleason
AMENITIES: 32 different kinds of pie, Cakes, Scones, Muffins, Coffee, Tea, & Hot Chocolate
LOCATION: Stars Hollow
