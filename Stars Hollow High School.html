Stars Hollow High School

About Stars Hollow High School

Their school colors are red and white, and the teams are the Minutemen. Stars Hollow High says the Pledge of Allegiance in 6 different languages. A sign outside of the school establishes the school as "Division Champs, 1997-98". Hallway signs indicate that the school has a French club and Germany club. Typical of many high schools, the school also has a marching band and a cheerleader squad, both of which Lane is a member of in her time at the school.
