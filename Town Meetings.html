Town Meetings

About Town Meetings

In Stars Hollow, the Mayor assembles the members of the town here to discuss upcoming events ranging from the Thanksgiving Pilgrim Festival to the re-zoning of parking spaces.[ On many occasions, the people of Stars Hollow are summoned to the town meeting, where Taylor informs his citizens of current goings on and conducts polls on important town decisions.

TYPE: Legislative democratic process
DATE: Thursday evenings, 8 o'clock
HOST(S): Taylor Doose & Miss Patty (assistant)
LOCATION: Miss Patty's School of Ballet
OCCURENCE(S): Weekly
