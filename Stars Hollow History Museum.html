Stars Hollow History Museum

About Stars Hollow History Museum

The Stars Hollow History museum comprises possible historical documentation, which Taylor Doose had trouble verifying. Thus it was built as a diorama taking visitors through speculative Stars Hollow history.

BUSINESS: Stars Hollow History Museum
TYPE: Museum
PROPRIETOR: Stars Hollow Historical Society
Est: 2005
History: The former house of Joshua Twickham, who willed it to the Stars Hollow Historical Society. It was converted into a dioarama presentation.
STAFF: Luke Danes, Kyle, Gypsy, Dean Forester, Kirk Gleason, Taylor Doose
AMENITIES: Dioarama
LOCATION: Stars Hollow
