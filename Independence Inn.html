Independence Inn

About Independence Inn

It was owned by Mia Bass until she sold it in 2003, after the inn was severely damaged by a terrible fire. The Independence Inn hosted many events in its dining hall, such as fashion shows, weddings, the famous Bracebridge Dinner and the annual gathering of the Edgar Allen Poe Society.

BUSINESS: Independence Inn
TYPE: Hotel
PROPRIETOR: Mia Bass (until 2003)
STAFF: Lorelai Gilmore (manager), Michel Gerard (assistant manager), Sookie St. James (head chef), Tobin (night manager), Derek
AMENITIES: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Events, Gardens
LOCATION: Stars Hollow
