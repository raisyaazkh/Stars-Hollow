Bid On A Basket

About Bid On A Basket

This is a annual fundraiser where an individual makes a basket. This basket is then bid on at the Bid on A Basket fundraiser and sold to the highest bidder. This bidder then goes on a "date" with the basket maker for a picnic, consuming the contents of the basket.

TYPE: Fundraiser
DATE: Winter
HOST(S): Stars Hollow
LOCATION: Town Square
OCCURENCE(S): Annual
