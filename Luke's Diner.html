Luke's Diner

About Luke's Diner

Luke's Diner is a busy eatery situated owned by Luke Danes, who inherited his father's Hardware Store and turned it into a diner. The diner is popular and a mainstay for the townspeople. Luke's coffee particularly receives high praise.

TYPE: Restaurant
PROPRIETOR: Luke Danes
Est: between 1990-1992
History: William's Hardware (formerly)
MANAGER(S): Luke Danes
STAFF: Caesar (chef), Rachel (wait staff), Jess Mariano (Former-wait staff), Froggy (Former-wait staff), Lane Kim (wait staff), Zack Van Gerbig (substitute for Lane)
AMENITIES: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Pies, Donuts, Soft drinks, Milkshakes
LOCATION: Stars Hollow
