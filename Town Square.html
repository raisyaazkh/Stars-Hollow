Town Square

About Town Square

The town square in Stars Hollow is the scene of many events and festivals, including Easter Egg hunts, the Bid On A Basket fundraiser, the Autumn Festival, and the Hay Bale Maze. It has a central gazebo, and is bordered by Doose's Market, Kim's Antiques, among other businesses, plus the post office and a church.
